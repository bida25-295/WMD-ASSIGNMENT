About Kylie Cosmetics

Empowering beauty, confidence, and self-expression since day one

Our Story

Kylie Cosmetics, founded by Kylie Jenner in 2015, is a leading American cosmetics brand known for its innovative products and strong social media presence.

Kylie Cosmetics is a global beauty brand offering high-quality makeup and skincare products.

Our mission is to empower individuals to express themselves through beauty. We believe that makeup is more than just products — it's a form of self-expression, creativity, and confidence.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity for all skin tones and types
  • High-performance, cruelty-free formulas
  • Innovation in beauty technology
  • Empowering the next generation of beauty lovers
